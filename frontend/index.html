Early Blight

Caused by Alternaria solani fungus. Early detection allows for effective treatment.

Confidence Level 80.2%
Key Symptoms
  • Small, dark brown to black spots on older leaves
  • Spots enlarge to form concentric rings (target spots)
  • Yellowing of leaves around the spots
  • Leaves may wilt and die prematurely
  • Lesions may also appear on stems and tubers
Prevention Methods
  • Practice 3-4 year crop rotation with non-solanaceous crops
  • Use certified disease-free seed potatoes
  • Space plants properly for good air circulation
  • Avoid overhead irrigation to keep foliage dry
  • Remove and destroy infected plant debris
Treatment Recommendations
  • Apply fungicides containing chlorothalonil or mancozeb
  • Use copper-based fungicides as preventive measure
  • Apply treatments at 7-10 day intervals during favorable conditions
  • Remove severely infected plants to prevent spread
  • Ensure proper nutrition to help plants resist infection
